About Me

I'm a driven researcher with a passion for leveraging technology to tackle pressing healthcare and environmental challenges. With a proven track record in leading and contributing to impactful projects, I've spearheaded initiatives funded by prestigious organizations like the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India and the University of California, USA.
